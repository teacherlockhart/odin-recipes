Nachos

Cover corn tortilla chips with shredded cheddar cheese and put it in the oven to melt the cheese.

  • corn chips
  • 2 C shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 C diced olives
  • 1/2 C Jalepenos
  • 1 C Pico de Gallo
  1. Pre-heat oven to 275F.
  2. Put corn chips on an oven-safe plate.
  3. Cover corn chips with 1 and 1/2 cups of shredded cheese spread evenly over chips.
  4. Put chips and cheese in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread rest of cheese, jalepenos, olives and pico de gallo on the chips.
  6. (Optional) Add cilantro and sour cream to garnish.
